About the Workshop

The workshop organized by the Computer Science and Engineering Department aims to provide students with hands-on experience in various cutting-edge technologies and tools. Through interactive sessions and practical exercises, participants will gain valuable skills and insights that are relevant to the industry.

Workshop Topics

  • Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence
  • Blockchain Technology
  • Cybersecurity and Ethical Hacking
  • Internet of Things (IoT) and Embedded Systems

Workshop Schedule

The workshop will be conducted over a span of two days, with each day focusing on different topics and practical sessions. Participants are required to bring their laptops for hands-on exercises.

  • Day 1: Introduction to Machine Learning and AI (Morning Session)
    Hands-on Lab: Building a Simple Neural Network (Afternoon Session)
  • Day 2: Understanding Blockchain Technology (Morning Session)
    Hands-on Lab: Implementing Smart Contracts (Afternoon Session)
