About the Program

The CSE Department has well established laboratories with good computing facilities, software tools and applications to provide practical exposure to students. The following labs are set up in the Department in line with technology trends: Mobile and IoT laboratory, Cisco laboratory, Oracle laboratory, Cloud and Data Analytics laboratory, and SAP laboratory. We conduct Value Added Courses, Hands-on workshops and Technical Talks throughout the academic year in order to bridge the gap between the industry and the institute. Conducting the above mentioned events help the students to enhance their skill set. Our students regularly participate in Design Contests and Conferences and win prizes. The placement for CSE has been excellent with an average salary of 6.52 Lakhs per annum. Our recruiters include top companies such as Arcesium, Amazon, Accenture, CTS, Caterpillar, Citrix, HP Enterprise (R&D), IVTL Infoview, L&T, Oracle, Qualcomm, Societe Generale, TCS and Zoho. Students are encouraged and motivated to do Mini Projects, Online Courses such as NPTEL, Coursera, Udemy, Udacity and Internships which are very much helpful during campus recruitment season.
