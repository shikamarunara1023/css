B.E. ELECTRONICS AND COMMUNICATION ENGINEERING


About the Program

The ECE department serves over 250 undergraduate students with a team of dedicated faculty members who are committed towards teaching and research in the areas of Signal processing, Electronic Circuit design, Communication Systems, VLSI, Machine Learning, Internet of Things (IoT)and Embedded Systems. They motivate the students to do real time projects and contribute to the society. The department has fully equipped laboratories with sophisticated equipments for conducting laboratory courses, research and consultancy activities. The collaboration with industries such as National Instruments, Keysight Technologies, Texas Instruments and PASCO has aided the department in providing a good platform for the faculty and students to learn the concepts experientially and to work on real-time industrial problems.

Key Insights

  • A fine blend of highly experienced and young faculty members, both doctorates and those pursuing research
  • Use of various active learning methods and aids to enable joyful learning and a better understanding
  • Invited lectures and value added courses offered by industry personnel on recent trends to bridge the gap between academia and industry practices
  • Industrial visits, in-plant-trainings and internships during end semester vacations to bridge the gap between industry and academia

Mission

  • To design a curriculum focusing the future industry needs and evolutions and society as a whole
  • To offer infrastructure and learning environment comparable to global standards to provide experiential learning
  • To collaborate with alumni and academic experts and establish Industry Institute Interactions to equip the students to apply engineering principles for their holistic development
  • To promote innovative research in cutting edge technologies and indigenous product/process development through multidisciplinary approach
