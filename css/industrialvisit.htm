About the Industrial Visit

The industrial visit organized by the Computer Science and Engineering Department offers students the opportunity to gain practical insights into the functioning of various industries related to computer science and engineering. It provides students with exposure to real-world applications of the concepts learned in the classroom.

Industrial Visit Locations

  • Software Development Companies
  • IT Service Providers
  • Research and Development Centers
  • Data Centers and Cloud Computing Facilities

Benefits of Industrial Visit

  • Understanding the practical aspects of software development and deployment
  • Exploring the latest technologies and trends in the industry
  • Interacting with industry professionals and experts
  • Gaining insights into career opportunities in computer science and engineering
